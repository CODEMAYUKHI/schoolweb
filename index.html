About us

St. Joseph's High School, Matigara, affiliated to the Council for Indian Schools Certificate Examinations, aims at the education of the Catholic Community around, and extends its services to the members of other communities to the extent possible.

Time when we started our Journey

St. Joseph’s School began on 15th February, 1961 at the request of the guardians and parents of Siliguri town who had made a written appeal to His Grace, Archbishop Vivian Dyer of Calcutta for a missionary school. Initially the school was located in Sharaf Bhavan, a rented building in the heart of Siliguri Town.
